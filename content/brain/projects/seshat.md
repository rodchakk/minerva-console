# Seshat

Financial operating system for freelancers, developers, and small studios.

## Overview

Seshat is a fintech product under the Minerva umbrella. It manages invoicing, payments, and financial workflows for independent professionals and small teams.

## Boundaries

- Seshat has its own Supabase project and operational database.
- Brain does not store Seshat operational data.
- Brain may reference Seshat product decisions and architecture.

## Status

- **Status:** Approved
- **Stage:** Active development
- **Infrastructure:** Supabase (dedicated project)
